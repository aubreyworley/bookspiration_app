Bay Area Bookspiration


Read. Explore. Learn. Grow.

Bay Area Bookspiration is a local book club that strives to inspire, motivate & encourage members to become avid readers through group interaction.

The local chapter welcomes everyone, including those not local, to join the group read along and come to meet-ups in person or online as we embark on shared book explorations.

Our Mission

As our world is ever-changing and ever-connecting, we seek to encourage people to continue to grow personally, professionally, socially, and intellectually through reading books of all kinds. The more variety we expose ourselves to, the more we can adapt to our environments, relate to others of different backgrounds, and discover new worlds. Who doesn't want to time travel, experience the lives of history's greatest, and jump into new tales of magic and mystery?
